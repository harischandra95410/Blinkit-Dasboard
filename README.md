# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of Blinkit (India's Last Minute App) using Power BI. The dashboard provides valuable insights into sales performance, customer satisfaction, inventory distribution, and outlet performance. The objective is to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions by visualizing key business metrics and identifying growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution using Power BI dashboards and KPI-driven visualizations.

The analysis focuses on:

* Sales performance across different product categories
* Customer satisfaction metrics
* Outlet performance evaluation
* Inventory distribution analysis
* Geographic sales trends
* Revenue optimization opportunities

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

| KPI             | Description                                   |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Total Sales     | Overall revenue generated from all items sold |
| Average Sales   | Average revenue generated per transaction     |
| Number of Items | Total count of items sold                     |
| Average Rating  | Average customer rating across products       |

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Total Sales by Fat Content

**Objective:** Analyze the impact of fat content on overall sales.

**Visualization:** Donut Chart

Metrics Included:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Rating

---

### 2. Total Sales by Item Type

**Objective:** Identify top-performing item categories based on sales.

**Visualization:** Bar Chart

Metrics Included:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Rating

---

### 3. Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales

**Objective:** Compare sales performance across outlets segmented by fat content.

**Visualization:** Stacked Column Chart

Metrics Included:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Rating

---

### 4. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment Year

**Objective:** Evaluate how outlet age influences sales performance.

**Visualization:** Line Chart

---

### 5. Sales by Outlet Location

**Objective:** Analyze geographic distribution of sales.

**Visualization:** Funnel Map

---

### 6. Outlet Type Performance Analysis

**Objective:** Provide a complete comparison of all outlet types.

**Visualization:** Matrix Card

Metrics Included:

* Total Sales
* Average Sales
* Number of Items
* Average Rating
